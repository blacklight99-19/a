Everything I got and everything I learned was a gift from my dear ones, my parents, cousins, friends & others. So I  thought it would be a great idea to give what I make as gifts & receive what I need as gifts. So I have a gifts page where I put all my gifts that you might like and I will have another page where there would be things that I will need. You could gift me what I need or help me get my gifts by paying for the things you take.
